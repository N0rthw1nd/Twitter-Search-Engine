Marvel Entertainment Group, Inc. (Marvel or MEG), incorporated and included Marvel Comics and Marvel Productions.
Stan Lee, original name Stanley Martin Lieber, American comic book writer best known for his work with Marvel Comics. Among the hundreds of characters and teams that he helped to create were the Fantastic Four, Spider-Man, the Avengers, and the X-Men.
The books joined a roster of increasingly successful series that also included The Incredible Hulk. Lee and Kirby added another winner to the group when they created The X-Men.
Other Lee creations to receive the Hollywood treatment were Daredevil  Hulk , and Iron Man . Lee was often featured in cameo roles in those films, a tradition that continued after Disney purchased Marvel in . A flood of Marvel films followed the purchase, including those based on Lee-Kirby creations such as Thor , the Avengers , and Ant-Man . In November  Lee filed a $10 million lawsuit against Marvel after failing to receive any profits from the first Spider-Man movie, and in the court ruled in favour of Lee.
